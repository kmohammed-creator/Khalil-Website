1. Close the accounting period.
2. Review management accounts and variance reports.
3. Investigate variances over threshold.

6. Escalate material risks to Trust Board if required.

How-To Guide: Incident Reporting Procedure

1. Make the area safe immediately.
2. Inform senior leadership.
3. Complete incident form within 24 hours.
4. Notify Compliance Education if required.
5. Investigate and document findings.
6. Implement corrective actions.

How-To Guide: Managing an Operational Escalation
